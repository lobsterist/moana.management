Boutique Strategic Consulting

Profitable growth for services and SaaS companies in IT.

Moana Management is a boutique management consultancy serving professional services and SaaS companies in the IT segment — consultancies, agencies, software services firms, and software product businesses. We help their founders, executives, and boards turn growth into profitable growth: sound strategy, a healthy commercial model, and an organisation built to sustain both.

What we do

We work with a small number of IT services and SaaS companies on the questions that decide whether growth creates value: where to play, how to win, and how to make the business model earn its keep.

Growth Strategy

Sharpening direction for services firms and SaaS businesses — positioning, portfolio, target customers, and internationalisation — so the pipeline fills with the right work at the right price.

Profitability & Operating Model

Turning revenue into results: pricing and commercial models, utilisation and delivery economics in services, recurring-revenue economics in SaaS, and the operating discipline to scale without margins eroding.

Executive & Board Advisory

A trusted sparring partner for CEOs, leadership teams, and boards of services and software companies. Independent perspective on the decisions that are difficult to discuss anywhere else.

A deliberately small firm

Moana Management is not a scaled consultancy — by design. Every engagement is led and delivered personally by the firm's founder, a senior advisor with decades of executive experience building, leading, and growing professional services and software companies in the IT industry.

  • Senior counsel only. You work directly with the principal — no leverage model, no junior teams, no hand-offs.
  • Few engagements at a time. We take on a limited number of clients so each one gets genuine attention.
  • Operator's perspective. Advice grounded in real experience running services and software businesses, not just analysing them.
  • Candour over comfort. Clients hire us for the truth, plainly stated, and recommendations we would act on ourselves.
