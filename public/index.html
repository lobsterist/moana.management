Moana Management

Profitable growth for professional services companies in IT, and SaaS.

Moana Management is a boutique strategic consultancy. I advise the founders, executive teams, and boards of consultancies, agencies, software services firms, and SaaS businesses — on strategy, on the commercial model, and on the decisions that are difficult to discuss anywhere else.

The firm is deliberately small. Every engagement is led and delivered personally, grounded in decades of executive experience building, leading, and growing these businesses. Few clients at a time. Candour over comfort.

The counsel is grounded in research as well as practice: the firm's principal is co-author of the Mearra Method and a PhD researcher on how to run software projects better than agile allows.

I. Growth strategy

Positioning, portfolio, target customers, and internationalisation — so the pipeline fills with the right work at the right price.

II. Profitability & operating model

Turning revenue into results: pricing and commercial models, utilisation and delivery economics in services, recurring-revenue economics in SaaS, and the operating discipline to scale without margins eroding.

III. Executive & board advisory

An independent sparring partner for the decisions that matter most — a senior perspective with no agenda beyond the truth, plainly stated.
